3 ways to find your Steam profile

1
Copy your profile URL (easiest)
Open Steam → click your name (top right) → View my profile → copy the URL from your browser's address bar. It will look like https://steamcommunity.com/id/yourname or https://steamcommunity.com/profiles/7656…
2
Use the Steam app
Open Steam → click your name → Account details → your SteamID64 is the long number in the URL.
3
Use steamid.io
Go to steamid.io, search your Steam name, and copy the steamID64 number.
⚠️ Important: Your Steam display name (what friends see) is not the same as your profile URL. Don't type your display name — paste the full URL or ID number.
